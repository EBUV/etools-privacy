ETools – Reassign Circuits

Version: 1.0.0

This plugin allows you to reassign selected electrical circuits to another panel in Autodesk Revit.

How to Use

  1. Select one or more elements connected to an electrical circuit in the model or in the system browser.
  2. Click Reassign on the ETools tab, under the Circuits panel.
  3. A dialog box will appear, allowing you to choose the target panel for reassignment.
  4. Confirm your selection. The selected circuits will be moved to the new panel.

Requirements

  • Revit 2023, 2024, 2025, or 2026
  • MEP discipline with electrical systems
